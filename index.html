Suvida Oral Contraceptive Pill

Suvida birth control pill, the flagship brand of Eskag Pharma, is primarily used as a method of preventing pregnancy. As one of the best contraceptive pills in india, Suvida offers a 28-day pack consisting of 21 low-dose hormonal pills and 7 iron tablets.

Each of the 21 hormonal white pills contains Levonorgestrel 0.15 mg and Ethinyl estradiol 0.03 mg. These hormones work together to inhibit ovulation (the release of eggs from the ovaries) and avoid pregnancy. By combining two hormones: estrogen and progestin, it ensures effective birth control.

By preventing ovulation, Suvida oral contraceptive pill effectively reduces the chances of fertilization and pregnancy. Each of the 7 iron pills contain Ferrous Fumarate 60 mg. This formulation offers effective contraception and includes iron supplementation to support women’s health.

Safe Usage Guide

Is Suvida Right For You?

Understanding who may benefit from Suvida and when medical advice should be sought before starting oral contraceptive pills.

Who Can Use Suvida?

Suvida supports women seeking reliable family planning under appropriate medical guidance.

  • Women with or without children
  • Married women planning child spacing
  • Unmarried women seeking contraception
  • Women up to 45 years of age
  • Women recovering after miscarriage
  • Women with Endometriosis
  • Women with Premenstrual Syndrome

Who Should Avoid It?

Certain medical conditions require consultation with a healthcare professional before using Suvida.

  • Suspected pregnancy
  • Breastfeeding (first 6 months)
  • Recent childbirth
  • Heart disease
  • Severe hypertension
  • Liver disease
  • Heavy smoking after age 35

Medical Guidance

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting or changing any oral contraceptive medication.

28 Day Guide

How To Take Suvida

Follow a simple 28-day routine for reliable and effective contraception. Taking one pill every day at the same time helps maximize effectiveness.

1

Start on Day 5

Begin your first white pill on the 5th day of your menstrual cycle.

2

21 White Pills

Take one white pill every day for the next 21 days.

3

7 Iron Tablets

Continue with one brown iron tablet daily for 7 days.

4

Start New Pack

Begin the next Suvida strip immediately without taking a break.
